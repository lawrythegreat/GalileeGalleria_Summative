About Galilee Galleria

Welcome to Galilee Galeria, a website which showcases a diverse collection of works from passionate creators, featuring projects in video production, graphic design, and visual storytelling. We aim to inspire and connect through creativity, highlighting innovative ideas and artistic expression in every piece. Whether it’s motion, color, or concept, each work reflects our commitment to pushing creative boundaries and bringing visual art to life.

Nika Santiago

Nika transforms ideas into compelling visuals that reflect creativity and purpose. Whether she’s designing brand identities, social media graphics, or visual layouts, Nika’s work is driven by innovation and a dedication to delivering high-quality, impactful results.

Lawrence Tapuz

Lawrence is a creative video and film enthusiast with a passion for storytelling through moving images. He enjoys capturing moments, emotions, and ideas that connect with audiences on a deeper level.

Khea Eder

Khea is a passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for visual storytelling and detail. She specializes in creating bold, modern, and meaningful designs that connect with audiences across digital and print media.
